i want to create a simple app game that you can play with your friends locally. the game works by sending out a url that all of the friends can click on to play as a party-like game. the game works like a vibe meter guesser. the game will start by prompting each person to type in two opposite phrases, such as "green flag or red flag", "cheating or not cheating", "asian or asian american", etc. everyone will see what everyone else has entered. these two unique phrases are meant to be polar opposites of each other. after everyone has finished their opposite phrases, the game then begins a turn with assigning one of these opposite phrases an integer between 1-100, where 1 is one end of the phrase, and 100 is the opposite end of the phrase (such as 1 to green flag, and 100 to red flag). one of the people will be assigned the task as "the vibe man" where they will enter a short story that most closely matches the random value they are given by the game. for example, if the value was 80 and the opposite phrase was "green flag as 1 or red flag as 100", then the vibe man would give a short story like "they're slightly mean as a person with a tendency to eat your food". for everyone else in the game, their task is to guess this random value between 1-100 based on the short story they are given. if they guess within 3 integer values of the real value then they awarded 3 points, within 4 integer values then they are awared 2 points, within 5 integer values then they are awarded 1 point, anything outside of this range they are not awarded any points. if nobody gets a perfect score of 3 points, then a new person is assigned the task as the vibe man and a new random value. this cycle repeats until at least one person gets a perfect score or everyone has been the vibe man once in this cycle for this opposite phrase. once the turn of this opposite phrase is concluded, the game will repeat onto the next opposite phrase, until everyone has played. lets work on a localhost development for this project
